The Dynamic Task Manager

Manage your tasks efficiently and efficiently!

1: Enter a task in the input field and click "Add Task" to add it to your list.
2: To delete a specific task, select it from the dropdown and click "Delete Task".
3: Mark tasks as completed by clicking on them in the task list.
4: To clear all tasks, click the "Clear All" button.
5: Your tasks will be displayed below the buttons for easy reference.
